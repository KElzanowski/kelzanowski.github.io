My work explores projects in which I aim to boost my skill and knowledge of website and user interface design, by becoming a wizard in this specific craft. This is completed through connecting my hobbies and interests with website, branding, and infographic designs that inform, tell stories, and promote values. All topics featured in my exhibition, shape me as a person, and solidify my future career ambitions.

Shaping Connections Through Video Games

An interactive narrative website concept, focusing on how friends and families connect through their favourite video game stories and memories. Created as a digital archive, for people who believe video games are a waste of time, despite forming relationships and memories! Click the arrows on the image to scroll through.

ProVisual Brand and Web Design

ProVisual, an e-sports themed game production service and brand for young procrastinators. Modern logo and clean website design inspired by Twitch, and created using Illustrator and Figma.

The Evolution of Website Design Brochure

An informative brochure about the evolution of website design, designed like a fold-out paper mobile phone! Created for designers and tech enthusiasts who'd like to learn a bit more about how the web has visually evolved over the years.

Ukraine Crisis Website Infographic

Conceptual website infographic around supporting and donating to aid Ukrainians in need during the war. Eye-catching design with the names of charities anyone can donate to.

Successful UIUX Design Posters

Dual-themed poster infographics featuring tips and tricks for successful user interface and experience design, for beginner web designers and developers.

Koffee Co. Brand and App Design

Koffee Co., a cosy and organic friendly coffee shop brand design, and mobile app with focus on user interface and experience, sketched, then designed in Illustrator. Inspired by Coffee #1, Costa, and Kona Coffee.
